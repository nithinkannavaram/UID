The Tech Quiz

Neurons firing. Data processed. The ultimate quiz showdown.

About the Event

The Tech Quiz is the premier intellectual battle of Ignite '26. We're testing everything from the deep history of Silicon Valley legends to the cutting-edge developments in AI, Quantum Computing, and Web3. Do you have the processing power to beat the best minds?

Hosted by a renowned quizmaster, this event features multiple rounds of rapid-fire questions, visual puzzles, and buzzer-beating thrillers.

Tournament Format

  • Prelims — Written test containing 25 high-difficulty tech questions.
  • Top 6 Finale — Multi-stage stage round including Buzzer Round, Infinite Bounce, and Rapid Fire.
  • Tie Breaker — Sudden death question for any matching scores.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of exactly 2 members.
  • No usage of mobile phones or any external electronic devices during the rounds.
  • Quizmaster's decision is final and binding on all matters.
  • Negative marking applies in some specific rounds (to be announced on the spot).
  • Inter-college teams are allowed.
  • In case of a tie in prelims, the team with fewer incorrect answers in specific "star" questions wins.
