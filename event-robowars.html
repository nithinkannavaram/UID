RoboWars

Metal clashes. Sparks fly. Only the strongest machine survives.

About the Event

RoboWars is the ultimate test of mechanical engineering, strategy, and raw power. Teams design and build combat robots that face off in a custom-built battle arena.

Watching two robots tear each other apart in a gladiator-style arena is the most adrenaline-pumping spectacle of the fest. Flames, flippers, spinners, and crushers — anything goes!

Robot Specifications

  • Maximum weight: 15 kg (with battery)
  • Maximum dimensions: 50cm × 50cm × 50cm
  • Wireless control mandatory (2.4 GHz recommended)
  • Battery voltage must not exceed 24V
  • Pneumatic and hydraulic weapons allowed with safety clearance

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 3 members maximum
  • Each match is 3 minutes in the arena
  • KO (immobilization for 10 seconds) wins the round
  • No flame-based weapons or liquid projectiles
  • Entanglement devices (nets, ropes) are banned
  • All robots must pass a safety inspection before competing
  • Judges' decision is final
