Rocket League Showdown

Boost. Fly. Score. Unleash the soccar chaos.

About the Event

Rocket League Showdown brings the high-octane excitement of car-based soccer to the big stage. Teams of 3 compete in a fast-paced tournament where aerial skills, tactical positioning, and explosive teamwork are the keys to victory.

From ceiling shots to epic saves, witness the most kinetic e-sports event of the fest. Every match is a Best-of-X series played on high-spec gaming PCs in our dedicated arena.

Tournament Format

  • Open Qualifiers — Best of 3 single elimination bracket.
  • Top 8 Playoffs — Double elimination format (Winners/Losers brackets).
  • Grand Finale — Best of 7 with a 1-game advantage for the winners' bracket finalist.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 3 players (1 substitute allowed).
  • Platform: PC (Steam / Epic Games Store).
  • Default settings: 5-minute match, Overtime enabled.
  • Game mode: Standard Soccar.
  • Arena: Random selection from competitive map pool.
  • Standard controllers and peripherals provided; custom peripherals allowed.
  • Any form of toxic behavior or unsportsmanlike conduct will lead to disqualification.
