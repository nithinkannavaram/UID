Battle of Bands

Amplifiers on. Crowd roaring. This is your moment of glory.

About the Event

Battle of Bands is the ultimate music showdown at College Fest 2026. Bands from colleges across India compete on the grand open-air stage under the stars.

Rock, pop, fusion, indie, metal — every genre is welcome. Professional-grade sound systems, stage lights, and a screaming crowd of 5,000+ create an unforgettable concert experience. Past headliners have included members of Indian Ocean and Nucleya's team as judges.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Bands of 3 to 6 members
  • Performance time: 10-15 minutes (including setup)
  • At least one original composition is mandatory
  • Basic drum kit, mic stands, and PA provided
  • Bands must bring their own instruments (guitars, bass, keyboards, etc.)
  • Judging based on Musicality, Originality, Stage Presence, and Crowd Engagement
  • Explicit/offensive lyrics lead to disqualification

What We Provide

  • Professional PA system with monitoring
  • 5-piece drum kit (Tama/Pearl)
  • Stage lighting and smoke effects
  • Professional sound engineer
  • Green room and backstage area
