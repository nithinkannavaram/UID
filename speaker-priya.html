👩‍🔬 Research Lead, ISRO

Dr. Priya Sharma

"Space Tech & India's Next Frontier"

About the Speaker

Dr. Priya Sharma is a distinguished aerospace engineer and current Research Lead at ISRO. Her groundbreaking work in deep-space propulsion mechanics has been instrumental to some of India's most ambitious lunar and interplanetary missions over the past decade.

An alumna of MIT and IISc, Dr. Sharma is a prominent voice for women in STEM, frequently mentoring young engineers. Her session will shed light on the rigorous research happening behind the scenes and the emerging role of private space startups in India.

Session Details

Join Dr. Sharma as they delve deep into "Space Tech & India's Next Frontier". This session will cover the latest trends, invaluable insights from their journey, and actionable advice for students looking to excel in the Aerospace & Deep Tech sector.
