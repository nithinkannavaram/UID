Infinite Echoes

Style meets legacy. Walk the ramp of tomorrow.

About the Event

Infinite Echoes is the flagship fashion extravaganza of Ignite '26. This year's theme, "Futuristic Heritage," challenges designers and models to blend the richness of traditional attire with the bold, unconventional styles of the future.

Expect a night of glamour, high-voltage music, and breathtaking visuals as teams from across the country compete for the top spot on the runway.

Tournament Format

  • Ramp Walk — Performance based on theme, walking style, and confidence.
  • Description Round — Explaining the design philosophy and material choices.
  • Final Face-off — Top 3 teams return for a final walking segment and judge Q&A.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 8-12 models and 2-3 crew members.
  • Theme: "Futuristic Heritage" must be strictly followed.
  • Background music must be submitted 24 hours in advance in MP3 format.
  • Maximum stage time per team: 8 minutes (including entry and exit).
  • No usage of fire, water, or hazardous props on stage.
  • Green rooms will be provided; teams responsible for their own makeup and accessories.
  • Judging criteria: Theme adherence, Creativity, Stage Presence, and Coordination.
