About The Fest

Our Vision

College Fest 2026 is the flagship annual cultural and technical festival that brings together the brightest minds, creative souls, and passionate performers under one roof. "Ignite the Future" is our call to action — pushing boundaries, sparking innovation, and celebrating talent.

🎨

Cultural

Dance battles, music concerts, art exhibitions, open mic nights, and theatrical performances.

💻

Technical

Hackathons, coding contests, robotics challenges, AI workshops, and startup pitches.

🏆

Sports

E-sports tournaments, cricket, basketball, athletics, and inter-college championships.

🍔

Food & Fun

Food stalls, carnival rides, VR experiences, photo booths, and a midnight DJ night.
