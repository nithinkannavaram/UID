Pixel Perfect

Design with empathy. Solve with precision. Build for impact.

About the Event

Pixel Perfect is an intensive UI/UX design challenge where participants solve real-world problems through user-centered design principles. We're looking for designers who can balance aesthetics with functionality and accessibility.

The event spans 8 hours, starting with a problem statement reveal and ending with a high-fidelity prototype presentation to a panel of expert product designers.

Tournament Format

  • Brief Reveal — Challenging problem statement provided on the spot.
  • Design Sprint — 8 hours to conduct research, wireframe, and prototype.
  • Final Pitch — 5-minute presentation of the design solution and prototype.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 1-2 designers.
  • Software allowed: Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.
  • All design assets must be created during the event.
  • Usage of pre-made UI kits is allowed only for basic components (buttons, inputs).
  • Prototypes must be interactive and reflect the user flow clearly.
  • Final submission: Link to the Figma/XD file and a PDF of the case study.
  • Judging criteria: User Research, Interaction Design, Visual Design, and Presentation.
