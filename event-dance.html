Dance Inferno

The stage is set. The lights are blazing. Show us your fire.

About the Event

Dance Inferno is the most electrifying cultural event at College Fest 2026. Dancers from across the country battle it out in solo, duet, and group categories on the grandest stage.

From classical to hip-hop, contemporary to folklore — every style finds its spotlight. Celebrity choreographers and Bollywood professionals form the jury panel. The energy is unmatched, the stakes are high, and the crowd never stops cheering.

Categories

  • Solo — Individual performances, 3-5 minutes
  • Duet — Pair performances, 4-6 minutes
  • Group — Teams of 5-12, 6-10 minutes

Rules & Guidelines

  • Music tracks must be submitted 24 hours before the event
  • No vulgarity or offensive content in music or choreography
  • Props are allowed but must be pre-approved
  • Costumes must be stage-appropriate
  • Judging based on Choreography, Expression, Synchronization, and Creativity
  • Time violations result in point deductions
