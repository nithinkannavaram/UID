Cinematic Magic

Frames of expression. Stories that move the soul.

About the Event

Cinematic Magic is the premier filmmaking competition of the festival. We invite young directors, cinematographers, and storytellers to showcase their vision through the lens. Whether it's a documentary, a mockumentary, or a fictional narrative, your film has a home here.

Shortlisted films will be screened in the Grand Auditorium for a live audience and a panel of professional filmmakers from the industry.

Tournament Format

  • Submission — Uploading the film to the official portal by the deadline.
  • Screening — Viewing sessions in the auditorium for the top 10 selections.
  • Q&A Round — Directors answer questions from the jury regarding their creative process.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 1-5 members (Crew size only; cast not counted towards limit).
  • Maximum duration: 5 minutes (including credits).
  • Original content only; copyrighted music or footage will lead to disqualification.
  • Formats: MP4 or MOV, 1080p resolution preferred.
  • Subtitles (English) are mandatory if the film is in any other language.
  • Films must have been made after January 2024.
  • Judging criteria: Storytelling, Technical Execution, Cinematography, and Acting.
