Street Art Graffiti

Campus as a canvas. Expression in every spray. Urban art unleashed.

About the Event

Street Art Graffiti is a live transformation of our campus environment. We provide the walls, the primers, and a theme – you bring the cans and the vision. This competition celebrates the raw energy of urban art and the power of public expression.

Teams will work on large-scale boards or designated campus walls to create a mural that reflects the festival's spirit. A masterclass by a renowned street artist will precede the main competition.

Tournament Format

  • Mural Concept Submission — Sketch and color palette review for approval.
  • Live Painting — 6 hours of continuous creative spraying and detailing.
  • Final Reveal — Walkthrough with the jury explaining the mural's story.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 2-4 artists.
  • Base primer and 10 basic spray colors provided for each team.
  • Additional custom tools and nozzles allowed.
  • Theme will be announced 2 hours before the start.
  • Safety gear (masks and gloves) provided and mandatory.
  • No usage of spray paint outside designated mural zones.
  • Judging criteria: Color Composition, Detail & Technique, Originality, and Theme Harmony.
