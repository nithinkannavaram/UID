Capture The Flag

Break the code. Find the flag. Rule the digital frontier.

About the Event

Capture The Flag (CTF) is an intense 12-hour cybersecurity competition designed to test your offensive and defensive hacking skills. From web exploitation and reverse engineering to cryptography and forensics, this event covers the full spectrum of modern digital security.

Participants will face a variety of challenges ranging from beginner to advanced levels. It's a race against time to solve tasks, harvest flags, and climb to the top of the leaderboard.

Tournament Format

  • Jeopardy Style — Multiple categories of challenges with varying point values.
  • Duration — 12 continuous hours of hacking.
  • Dynamic Scoring — Points decrease as more teams solve a challenge.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 2-3 members.
  • Laptops with necessary tools must be brought by the participants.
  • Internet access will be provided via the campus network.
  • No DDOSing the infrastructure or other teams.
  • No sharing flags or write-ups during the event.
  • Attacking the scoring server is strictly prohibited.
  • Judge's decision is final on clarify and disputes.
