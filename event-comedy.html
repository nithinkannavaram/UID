Master of Jokes

Punchlines of fire. Roars of laughter. One comedy king.

About the Event

Master of Jokes is the ultimate hunt for the funniest bone in the country. We're providing a spotlight, a microphone, and a massive audience – all you need is a set of punchlines that land! From observational comedy to satirical takes, every style is welcome.

Winners will not only take home cash prizes but also get a chance to open for a renowned celebrity comedian during the Star Night festivities.

Tournament Format

  • Open Mic Prelims — 3-minute set to impress the screening panel.
  • Audience Choice Round — Top 10 perform on the Mini Stage for a live crowd vote.
  • The Grand Finale — Top 5 perform a full 7-minute set for the main jury.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Individual participation only.
  • Maximum time for finale: 7 minutes (5 minutes for prelims).
  • Language: English, Hindi, or a mix (Hinglish).
  • Strictly NO offensive content targeting specific religions, castes, or sensitive political issues.
  • Planners or notes are allowed on stage but not recommended for high scores.
  • Audience interaction is encouraged but must remain respectful.
  • Judging criteria: Observational Humour, Delivery, Timing, and Audience Reaction.
