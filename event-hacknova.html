HackNova 2026

36 hours. One mission. Build something the world hasn't seen yet.

About the Event

HackNova 2026 is the flagship hackathon of College Fest – a 36-hour non-stop coding marathon where the brightest minds come together to build groundbreaking solutions. Whether it's AI, blockchain, IoT, or web3 – your innovation takes center stage.

Teams of 4 will tackle real-world problem statements provided by our industry partners. Top teams get to pitch directly to leading VCs and tech companies.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 4 members maximum
  • All code must be written during the hackathon period
  • Pre-existing libraries and frameworks are allowed
  • Hardware components will be provided at the venue
  • Teams must present a working demo + pitch deck
  • Judging based on Innovation, Technical Depth, UI/UX, and Impact
  • Plagiarism leads to immediate disqualification

Tracks

  • AI & Machine Learning — Build intelligent systems that learn and adapt
  • FinTech — Disrupt payments, banking, and financial inclusion
  • HealthTech — Innovate for healthcare accessibility and diagnostics
  • Sustainability — Create solutions for climate, energy, and waste
  • Open Innovation — Anything that pushes boundaries
