Valorant Championship

5v5. Tactical precision. Clutch plays. The ultimate campus showdown.

About the Event

The Valorant Championship is the premier e-sports event at College Fest 2026. Teams of 5 battle it out in Riot Games' tactical shooter in a LAN tournament format, streamed live on giant screens for thousands of spectators.

From high-IQ strategies to pixel-perfect aim, every round is a test of communication, reflexes, and nerves. Shoutcasters provide live commentary, and the crowd energy rivals professional esports tournaments.

Tournament Format

  • Group Stage — Round-robin within groups of 4
  • Quarterfinals — Best of 1 (top 2 from each group)
  • Semifinals — Best of 3
  • Grand Final — Best of 5

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 5 players + 1 optional substitute
  • All players must have a Riot Games account above Level 20
  • Minimum rank: Silver (verified at check-in)
  • PCs, monitors, and peripherals are provided
  • Players may bring their own mouse, keyboard, and headset
  • Map pool: All current competitive maps
  • Anti-cheat monitoring active; cheating = lifetime ban from all events
  • Match disputes handled by tournament admin (decision is final)
