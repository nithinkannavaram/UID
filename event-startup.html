Startup Pitch Arena

Got a world-changing idea? Pitch it. Get funded. Build the future.

About the Event

Startup Pitch Arena is where student entrepreneurs meet real investors. Present your startup idea—whether it's a prototype, MVP, or just a brilliant concept—to a panel of venture capitalists, angel investors, and industry leaders.

The top pitches get direct mentorship from founders of unicorn startups, potential seed funding, and incubation support from our partner accelerators. This isn't just a competition—it's a launchpad.

Pitch Format

  • Round 1 — 3-minute elevator pitch (shortlisting)
  • Round 2 — 7-minute detailed pitch + 5 min Q&A
  • Final Round — 10-minute full pitch with live demo + 10 min investor Q&A

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 1 to 3 members
  • Must have a pitch deck (10 slides max)
  • A working prototype or demo earns bonus points
  • The idea must be original and not an existing funded company
  • Judging: Problem-Solution Fit, Market Size, Team, Scalability, and Presentation
  • NDA protection available for innovative ideas
  • Previous startup experience is not required
